Invoices

Show hours worked by the caregiver or number of shifts completed by the caregiver. Checking into a shift qualifies as a shift completion.

Live-In

Live-in pay calculated by the hour will pay live-in shifts at the live-in day-rate and live-in shifts when the caregiver is clocked in less that 16 hours by the hour. Live-in pay calculated by the shift will pay the live-in rate for the live-in shift regardless of check-out time.

Overtime

Some states require overtime to be paid per day, if worked more than eight hours.
