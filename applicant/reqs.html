Essential Care Work Requirements

Prospective Team Member

Enclosed is a list of requirements needed to work with EssentialCare.

Please bring an original copy of the following documents at the time of interview.

  1. Drivers License, ID Card and/or passport
  2. Social Security Card
  3. Professional License, Certification(s) and Caregiver Courses
  4. Current CPR Card and First Aid
  5. Proof of Negative TB Test
  6. Proof of Car Insurance
  7. Finger Print Card

As part of your relationship with EssentialCare, you will be asked to complete a document allowing us to do a criminal background check. You may also be asked to submit a drug screening or alcohol impairment screening as a condition of working with EssentialCare to ensure a drug free workplace.

Expectations while assisting our clients are as follows

  1. No use of cell phone on EssentialCare/Client time
  2. No text messaging on EssentialCare/Client time
  3. No visitors of any kind while on EssentialCare/Client time
  4. Clients home is to be kept neat and clean as if we had never been there

If you do not have these documents or understand any of the 4 points, please let us know at the time of the interview. Failure to meet any of the above conditions will result in self-termination from an EssentialCare relationship.

We look forward to working with you.
